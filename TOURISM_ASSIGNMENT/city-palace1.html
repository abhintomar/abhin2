About FRI Palace

Forest Research Institute (FRI) Dehradun is a premier institution under the Indian Council of Forestry Research and Education (ICFRE)123. It was established in 1906 as Imperial Forest Research Institute1. FRI Dehradun is one of the country's oldest research institutes dedicated to forest research4. It is the most comprehensive forest-based training centre in India and attracts thousands of students each year to study and research every aspect of forests

History

Dietrich Brandis established the Forest Research Institute (FRI) in 1878. It was first known as the British Imperial Forest School.

In 1906, it was restored as the Imperial Forest Research Institute, located at Chand Bagh on Mall Road. Chand Bagh is home to the famed Doon School. In 1923, the institute stalwarts purchased a bigger campus at this site. After that, work on constructing the brand-new structures got underway. The primary structure, designed by CG Blomfield and completed in 1929, has a Greco-Roman architectural style. It is the place where most forest officers graduate from
